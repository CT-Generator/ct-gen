## ADDED Requirements

### Requirement: Five-question real-or-fake quiz

The system SHALL provide a quiz mode at `/quiz` that presents five short conspiracy descriptions and asks the user, for each, whether it describes a real (documented, broadly-acknowledged) historical conspiracy or a fake generated by the app. The quiz SHALL conclude with a score, the correct labels for each item, and a one-paragraph reflection that surfaces the educational point ("real conspiracies look unlike the typical 'just asking questions' theory").

#### Scenario: User completes a quiz
- **WHEN** a user starts a quiz from `/quiz`
- **THEN** the system displays five items in random order: between 3 and 4 fakes drawn from app-generated theories, and between 1 and 2 reals drawn from a curated historical set
- **AND** for each item the user answers REAL or FAKE
- **AND** at the end the system shows the user's score, reveals each correct answer, and shows a closing reflection paragraph

### Requirement: Curated, conservative real-conspiracy set

The "real" pool SHALL contain only conspiracies with unambiguous documentary record and broad consensus across mainstream historical sources. The v1 pool SHALL be exactly: Watergate, COINTELPRO, Iran-Contra, Tuskegee Syphilis Study, MK-ULTRA. Contested or current-events cases (e.g., JFK, Epstein, contemporary geopolitical events) MUST NOT appear.

#### Scenario: Quiz item pool audit
- **WHEN** a quiz is generated
- **THEN** every "real" item in the rendered quiz comes from the curated set: Watergate, COINTELPRO, Iran-Contra, Tuskegee, MK-ULTRA
- **AND** no contested historical or contemporary events appear as quiz items
- **AND** each "real" item's display text presents the historically established summary, not a partisan framing

### Requirement: Fake quiz items are condensed and labeled-as-fake post-hoc

Fake items SHALL be drawn from generations stored in the database. For quiz display, the four recipe-move sections SHALL be condensed into a single 80–120 word summary suitable for parallel reading with the historical real items. The condensation MUST preserve the conspiratorial voice but MUST NOT add to or smooth over the absurdity baked into the original generation.

#### Scenario: Fake item is condensed for quiz display
- **WHEN** a fake quiz item is selected from a stored generation
- **THEN** a deterministic per-generation condensation runs (cached) that produces an 80–120 word single paragraph
- **AND** the condensation preserves the four-move structure as flowing prose
- **AND** the original generation's permalink remains the canonical record

### Requirement: Post-quiz reflection

After scoring, the quiz SHALL display a closing reflection that names the educational point: real conspiracies are documented by paper trails, defectors, and prosecutions, not by "just asking questions" patterns. The reflection MUST link to the recipe page and to the about/credits page.

#### Scenario: User finishes quiz
- **WHEN** the user submits their fifth answer
- **THEN** the score, per-item correct labels, and a 100–200 word reflection paragraph are displayed
- **AND** the reflection links to `/recipe` and to `/about`

### Requirement: Quiz results are not shareable as artifacts

The quiz result page SHALL provide a permalink suitable for sharing, but MUST NOT generate a downloadable score image or PDF. Share buttons follow the always-link-back pattern.

#### Scenario: Share quiz result
- **WHEN** a user shares their quiz result
- **THEN** the share targets get a teaser plus the result permalink URL
- **AND** no downloadable image, PDF, or text artifact is produced
